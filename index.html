Tensorithm - AI Tarot Card Selector

This is a rudimentary tool, because as you'll see if you read the ReadMe in the [repo](https://github.com/krkwd/tensorithm-tarot), I'm not a developer. It can output 1 to 5 cards. No patterns. No Celtic Cross. But even with these limitations, there are some useful approaches to getting the guidance you seek.

A few examples:

  • Single Card Reading: Draw a single card to gain insight into a specific question or situation. The card represents the energies and guidance related to the inquiry. This can also be a YES/NO question, with the answer being up to the seeker's interpretation of the meaning and symbolism, and how it relates to the question
  • Two Card Reading: Present and future. Draw two cards to gain insight into the current situation, and guidance on how to move forward.
  • Two Card Reading: Forking paths. Draw two cards to gain insight into the current situation, and guidance on how to move forward.
  • Three Card Reading: Past, present, future. Past influences, present circumstances, future possibilities will be revealed.
  • Three Card Reading: Decision-making. Draw three cards to view three possible solutions to a problem or challenge. Each card represents a choice or aspect to consider, helping the seeker gain clarity.
    • Celtic
  • Five Card Reading:: Modified Celtic Cross. Draw five cards to represent different aspects of a situation, such as the present influences, challenges, guidance, and potential outcomes.
    • Your current state of being
    • Your current influence or obstacle
    • The origin of your influence of obstacle
    • The outcome if you follow your current path
    • The outcome if you follow the path the fifth card presents
Or come up with your own techniques. There are no wrong approaches to tarot as long as your question is clear and you are open to accepting the answers presented.
